About Tideline

Tideline is a descriptive macro regime tracker. It publishes a daily reading of cross-asset stress and trend conditions, computed from publicly available market data on a fixed schedule.

Every state Tideline assigns is logged permanently. Track-record performance — how often the readings line up with subsequent market behavior — is recorded continuously and surfaced on the dashboard over time.

Not investment advice. Tideline is a research and observation tool. Nothing it shows constitutes a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any security. Past readings do not predict future outcomes. You are responsible for your own positions.
